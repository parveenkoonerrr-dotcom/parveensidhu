Your Money Story

Know Your Numbers

Build your net worth statement and set up a budget that doesn't suck (I'll show you all of mine and how I got there). Learn to read your mortgage statement, understand amortization schedules, good debt vs. bad debt, interest rates, and your pay cheque — including why you contribute to EI and CPP, and how CPP will actually work when you retire.

Filing Taxes Without Crying

Screen recordings walking you through filing your own return, step by step. We'll go over deductions you're probably forgetting to give your accountant, and I'll show you how to set up and navigate your CRA My Account so the government feels less intimidating.

TFSA, RRSP, FHSA — Decoded

Which one, how much, in what order. The decision tree your accountant uses, given directly to you.

Investing for Real Life

Self-directed vs. robo-advisor — what to choose and why. Learn the basics of ETFs, MERs, how to read key ratios and a balance sheet. Open an account on camera, buy your first ETF, and set up auto-invest. Enough knowledge to make safe, informed investment decisions with confidence — not guesswork.

Side Hustles & Self-Employment
